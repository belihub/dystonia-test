This testimonial follows a guitarist's progress through video documentation. The initial clips show involuntary hand clenching when trying to play simple scale runs, followed by retraining sessions showcasing slow, deliberate, splint-assisted exercises.

The final progress video shows complete recovery, with the guitarist executing complex classical pieces effortlessly. The visual proof showcases the effectiveness of motor cortex remapping over months of structured practice.

> "Being able to watch the progress videos kept me motivated. Seeing the physical evidence of recovery is incredibly powerful."
