title: "Testimonial of a classical guitarist who overcame right hand index finger FD"
description: "The story of a classical guitarist who regained independent finger control and returned to playing concert repertoire."
---

This guitarist experienced severe clenching of the right-hand index finger, which threatened their career. After starting retraining sessions, we utilized customized splints to isolate the movement of the index finger and break the co-contraction patterns.

Within a few months of persistent, slow exercises, the involuntary curling resolved. The guitarist successfully returned to performing high-level classical concert repertoire with full speed and control.

> "After being told my condition was permanent, Yogi's structured retraining exercises gave me my right hand back. I am performing in concerts again."
