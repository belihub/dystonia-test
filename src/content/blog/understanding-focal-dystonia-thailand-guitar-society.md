description: "A summary of the talk given to guitarists in Thailand regarding early symptoms, prevention, and retraining fundamentals."
---

This article outlines the core slides and discussion points from my presentation to the Thailand Guitar Society. It covers the warning signs of focal hand dystonia, including minor coordination loss and subtle clenching that musicians often mistake for lack of practice.

The presentation emphasized early intervention, warning against the common mistake of 'playing through the block'. We discussed how pushing harder only deepens the abnormal neural pathways, and how immediately resting and utilizing ergonomic retraining is the safest route forward.

> "Never try to force through a dystonic block; doing so only strengthens the incorrect muscular habit in your brain."
