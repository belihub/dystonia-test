---
title: "Coping without retraining"
description: "Strategies, instrument modifications, and ergonomic changes to help you continue playing while managing FHD symptoms."
order: 5
---

For some musicians, starting a full course of movement retraining is not immediately feasible due to concert commitments or financial limits. This page details compensation strategies and ergonomic workarounds that allow you to continue playing with minimal distress.

We explore instrument setup alterations, repertoire selection, alternate fingering options, and focal splint techniques designed to bypass the dystonic block during performances, enabling you to sustain your career in the interim.

> "Coping techniques are valuable tools to manage performance schedules and keep playing while you prepare for a dedicated retraining period."
