This program is designed for musicians who want to start their retraining with structured, self-guided work complemented by monthly check-ins. It includes custom exercise plans, guidance on how to make your own practice splints, and progress metrics.

We focus on slow-speed movement isolation, postural correction, and sensory discrimination tasks. It provides a solid baseline for retraining without requiring constant direct oversight.

> "Ideal for self-disciplined players looking to set up their baseline retraining exercises with professional guidelines."
